# Privacy Policy — WSJ Audio Queue

Last updated: March 7, 2026

This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or browsing information. It does not use analytics, tracking, or third-party services.

All actions performed by this extension (opening WSJ pages, clicking buttons) occur entirely within your local browser session and solely on your behalf. No data ever leaves your device.

If you have questions, submit an issue at https://github.com/neo99/wsj/issues
